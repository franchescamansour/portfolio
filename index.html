OH ME?

I am a Graphic, Motion, Web, Print & Digital Designer

“I love turning ideas, into a reality and making little things turn into something huge”

—I just quoted myself

You can call me Frankie, or Coco.. or Franchesca, obviously. I speak 3 languages such as English, French and Arabic, oh and I've been trying to learn Spanish for the last 5 years but haven't gotten too far. I was basically born to do something that doesn't require reading books or writing exams. It just isn't me, which is why I am the one for you. Yes, you can say I'm a girly girl, but don't under estimate me, I really am a nerd on the inside with a million ideas ready to put to use. I love anything to do with art, and grew up wanting to impress others with my work. Which is why I chose to be a Graphic Designer. The perfect way to be creative and, let's be real, actually make some money. I've always liked the concept of business as well, so I guess it works. So let's talk and start building your concept into an actual business!

You most likely want to know a little about my history in the work field, I mean makes sense, you need to trust me right? I just completed my 3 years studying Graphic Design at Algonquin College which I won’t lie, was a long road, but feels better than ever to be done. I wouldn’t have this website up if it wasn’t for all the things I’ve learnt. I have experience working in the printing industry, learning anything and everything about print, pre-press, inks, designing for print, bindings, paper stock and much more. I also gained experience working at digital signage company, so anything related to design for digital screens, web design, social media etc. Two opposites right?

On my free time, I enjoy creating makeup videos, filming short travel films and editing them, and of course creating random graphic design pieces to add to my portfolio. My brain is usually everywhere all at once because of how passionate I am of what I do. That being said, I’d truly love to work with you and want to put my ideas to use, create for different industries, and most importantly, create the face of your current or new successful business!
