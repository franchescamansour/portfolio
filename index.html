SO YOU WANNA KNOW ABOUT ME

You can call me frankie, or coco.. or Franchesca, obviously. I was basically born to do something that doesn't require reading books or writing exams. It just isn't me, which is why I am the one for you. Yes, you can say I am a girly girl, but don't under estimate me, I really am a nerd on the inside with a million ideas ready to put to use. I love anything to do with art, and grew up wanting to impress others with my work. Which is why I chose to be a Graphic Designer. The perfect solution for creativity, combined with making real stuff happen. I've always liked the concept of business as well, so I guess it works. I love turning ideas, into a reality and making little things turn into something huge.
